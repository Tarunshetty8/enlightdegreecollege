About Enlight Degree College

Founded in 2005, Enlight Degree College has been a cornerstone of higher education in the region. Our journey began with a simple yet profound vision: to create a learning environment where students could not only achieve academic excellence but also develop the character, skills, and values necessary for a meaningful life.

Over the years, we have grown into a premier institution known for its innovative teaching methods, dedicated faculty, and a vibrant campus culture. We offer a diverse range of undergraduate programs designed to meet the evolving demands of the global workforce.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that empowers future leaders with knowledge, integrity, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating academic environment, foster critical thinking, and promote holistic development through a blend of rigorous academics and co-curricular activities.
